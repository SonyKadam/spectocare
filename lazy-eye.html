Lazy Eye Problem and related information

1. A condition in which one eye is unable to focus as clearly as the other.

2. Very common (More than 1 crore cases per year in India)

3. Treatment from medical professional advised

4. Often requires lab test or imaging

5. Can last several months or years

6. Common for ages 5 and younger

7. Family history may increase likelihood for some types

Symptoms are:-

1. An eye that wanders inwards or outwards

2. Eyes that appear to not work together

3. Poor depth perception

4. Blurred vision

5. Double vision

6. Squinting or shutting an eye

7. Head tilting

8. Abnormal results of vision or screening tests
